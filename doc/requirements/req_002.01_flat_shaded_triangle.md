# REQ-002.01: Flat Shaded Triangle

## Status

**Removed.** This requirement has been withdrawn from the specification.

## Rationale

Flat shading is not required by the design goals.
All triangles always interpolate vertex colors (Gouraud shading), depth, and texture coordinates.
Removing this requirement eliminates the GOURAUD=0 mode branch from the rasterizer pipeline and simplifies the implementation.

The parent requirement REQ-002 and the sibling requirements REQ-002.02 and REQ-002.03 remain in force.
Documents that previously referenced REQ-002.01 (UNIT-004, UNIT-005, UNIT-006, UNIT-007, INT-010) should treat those references as removed.
